Email: support@scanplanhvac.com Privacy Policy Effective date: March 21, 2026 Data collection. ScanPlanHVAC processes LiDAR scan data, room measurements, and building information solely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data or scan data on external servers. Third-party services. The app does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Camera & sensor access. The app requires access to your device's camera and LiDAR sensor to perform room scans, and to the compass for wall orientation data. This data remains on your device and is not shared. Data storage. All project data, scans, and reports are stored locally on your device. You control your data — deleting a project removes all associated data permanently. Children's privacy. This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children. Changes. We may update this policy from time to time.
